The Pleasant Mount School

Welcome to Pleasant Mount School, a non-sectarian educational institution in Summerville Subdivision, Mayamot, Antipolo City that has been nurturing well-rounded learners since 1982. Dedicated to excellence with complete facilities and a supportive learning environment, we strive to help every student grow academically and personally.

Mission Statement

The Pleasant Mount School commits herself to forming Pleasanites who live Christian and positive values, serve God and others, and who are academically equipped.

Vision Statement

The Pleasant Mount School envisions well-rounded citizens who are God fearing and academically prepared for higher learning.
